Cyber Security


Cyber Security protects computers or computer systems from cyber-attacks such as unwarranted access of information or data breeches. These measures can be put in place to protect the computers and their users. Malware spy ware or data breeches are just some of the unwarranted attacks or access gained to computers and their users. The Merriam–Webster dictionary defines cyber security as “measures taken to protect a computer or computer system (as on the Internet) against unauthorized access or attack” (Merriam-Webster, 2020).
